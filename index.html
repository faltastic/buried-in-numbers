Daily death count

In the month of July 2014 (till the 30th), the death of 1100 Palestinians have been confirmed by the Palestinian Ministry of Health.

"Over 5800 have been injured, including 822 children and 572 women, with some losing limbs and others disabled for life." -IMEMC

* The radial axis represents the total number of deaths in the highlighted areas.


Target areas

Gaza (red) and Khan Younis (green) saw the largest number of causalities.

The Shaujeeya neighbourhood in Gaza alone witnessed a massacre of sixty people on July 20.
