Data updated July 22, 2014 via IMEMC (International Middle East Media Center)

* The horizontal axis represents the day in July, and the vertical axis is the number of deaths in that day.


Daily death count

In the month of July 2014 (till the 21st), the death of 522 Palestinians have been confirmed by the Palestinian Ministry of Health.

"Over 3300 have been injured, including 822 children and 572 women, with some losing limbs and others disabled for life." -IMEMC

* The radial axis represents the total number of deaths in the highlighted areas.


Target areas

Gaza (red) saw the largest number of causalities followed by Khan Younis (green).

The Shaujeeya neighbourhood in Gaza alone witnessed a massacre of sixty people on 20 July.

* The horizontal axis represents the age, and the vertical axis is the number of deaths to a person of that age.


Young to old

More young Palestinans were buried than their older fellows, with the mid twenties seeing the greatest number of deaths.
